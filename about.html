Every piece
has a soul

Founded on a passion for timeless beauty, PUKHRAJ brings you handcrafted jewelry made with ethically sourced materials. Our journey began with a single vision: to transform raw minerals into symbols of enduring emotion.

For decades, our atelier has stood as a beacon of trust for families celebrating life's most precious moments. Every piece we create tells a unique story of meticulous craftsmanship, uncompromising quality, and the quiet authority of understated elegance.

The Pukhraj Pillars

Unwavering Trust

Built on generations of radical honesty, we ensure complete transparency in our sourcing. Every gemstone is traceable to its origin, honoring the earth and its people.

Guaranteed Purity

Every piece of jewelry is meticulously tested and hallmarked. We exclusively use 18k and 22k solid silver, ensuring your investment retains its brilliance and value for lifetimes.

Master Artisanship

Our karigars (master craftsmen) bring decades of experience, blending traditional heritage techniques with modern structural engineering to create works of wearable art.
