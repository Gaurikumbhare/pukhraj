OUR STORY

Every jewllry has a story

Founded on a passion for timeless beauty, PUKHRAJ JEWELLERS brings you handcrafted jewelry made with ethically sourced materials.

For years, our storefront opposite Siddheshwar Mangal Karyalaya on Manewada Road has stood as a beacon of trust for families celebrating life's most precious moments. Every piece we create tells a unique story of meticulous craftsmanship, uncompromising quality, and enduring elegance.

BEHIND THE SCENES

The Art of Jewelry Making

Creating a masterpiece is a journey. It begins with sourcing the finest, ethically-mined gemstones and purest metals from trusted suppliers around the world.

Our design team sketches intricate patterns inspired by India's rich cultural heritage. Then, our master craftsmen spend countless hours sculpting, setting, and polishing the piece entirely by hand.

The result? A breathtaking work of art that is ready to become a cherished part of your family's legacy.
